Safari on the River:
An African Experience

Sunday, September 18, 2016

Join us for a wonderful evening of food, wine, music and more. You'll also be helping to raise much needed funds for the education of children in Africa and the protection of wildlife.

Dress in your favorite Safari wear and join the fun at beautiful Juluka Landing as we celebrate African culture through music, dance, art, and food to raise money for The Africa Hope Fund's education outreach and environmental protection efforts.

4:00 PM to 7:00 PM
1951 Garden Highway, Sacramento, CA
